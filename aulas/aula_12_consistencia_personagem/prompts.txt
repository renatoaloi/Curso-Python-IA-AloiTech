# Aula 12 - Prompts Sugeridos

## Cena 1: O Mago no Estúdio/Biblioteca
A hyperrealistic photo of a powerful wizard, long dark robes, detailed ornate staff, standing in a mystical stone library filled with ancient books and glowing crystals, dramatic cinematic lighting, 8k resolution, sharp focus.

## Cena 2: O Mago em Ação (Poderes)
The same wizard in action, casting a powerful lightning spell, hands glowing with electric energy, dramatic pose, dark stormy sky in the background, epic fantasy art, cinematic 8k.

## Cena 3: O Mago no Cenário Épico (Montanha)
The same wizard standing on top of a mountain cliff at sunset, long cloak flowing in the wind, overlooking a vast fantasy kingdom, epic wide shot, golden hour lighting, cinematic 8k, photorealistic.
